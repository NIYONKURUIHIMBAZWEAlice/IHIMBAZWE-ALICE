Welcome to the Community Lost & Found

Lost something? Found something? Help others and get help by using our platform to report and find missing items.

We understand how stressful it can be to lose something important — or how frustrating it is not knowing how to return something you’ve found. That’s why we created this platform.

Our goal is to connect people who’ve lost items with those who’ve found them. Whether it’s a wallet, a phone, or a personal belonging, this platform helps you report and find items quickly and easily.

Start by reporting a lost or found item — or just scroll to explore more!
